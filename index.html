How It Works

INQUIRY
TO DELIVERY

A transparent export process built for buyers who need reliable biomass at scale — no surprises at the port.

01

Submit Inquiry

Tell us your product, volume, destination port, and target calorific specs. Use the form below or email us directly.

02

Formal Offer

Within 48 hours we send a detailed commercial offer with FOB/CIF pricing, spec sheet, and delivery timeline.

03

Pre-Shipment Inspection

Samples dispatched for approval. Third-party inspection via SGS or Intertek arranged upon request or as standard.

04

Production & Loading

Production commences on confirmed PO and payment terms. Bulk vessel or containerised, stuffed at origin port.

05

Docs & Delivery

Full docs — B/L, COA, packing list, phytosanitary, ISCC — sent digitally within 3 days of vessel departure.
